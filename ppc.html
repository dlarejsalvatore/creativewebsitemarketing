FAQs

Learn more about our PPC management services.

Do you require contracts?

We recommend starting with a 3-month minimum, but we are very confident in our work and offer month-to-month options. Longer-term contracts benefit clients with discounted monthly management fees.

How do pricing and payments work?

Our management fee is a flat monthly rate, quoted based on the campaign types and number of platforms. You pay the actual ad spend directly to Google, and we simply charge the management fee at the first of every month.

How can I quickly estimate a monthly media spend?

If you need to generate leads using search ads on Google for example, you can follow this formula below. We help determine custom estimates during the set-up process. (25 leads per month x avg CPC of $2.41) / .0375 avg CVR = $1,606

What type of communication and reporting can I expect?

You will have 24/7 access to a real-time reporting dashboard through Google Data Studio. Customized to your campaign objectives, this report will showcase key metrics and KPIs. You can expect to meet with us regularly to review campaign performance, discuss strategy, and provide recommendations for optimization. We believe in transparency and offer world-class customer service if questions or issues arise between meeting dates.

Which PPC platforms do you use?

Creative Website Marketing manages campaigns on the major PPC platforms, including Google Ads, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn and Microsoft Advertising. Our team is Google Ads certified with specializations in search, display and video ads.

Unsure which platform is the right fit for your advertising needs? That’s completely fine. As part of our strategy-first approach, we can recommend which platforms will be most effective based on your audience and your goals.

What landing page will be used?

We’ve found the best results come from using relevant pages on your website, for an authentic user experience. Custom landing pages can be designed as needed. We also provide landing page recommendations and solutions to improve conversion rates, in order to get the most out of your PPC campaigns as possible.
