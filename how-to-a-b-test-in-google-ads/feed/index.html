According to Google, drafts and experiments are only available for Search and Display Network campaigns. You won’t be able to create a draft or experiment for Video, App or Shopping campaigns.

If are running Search and/or Display campaigns but still don’t see “Drafts and Experiments,” you may need to click the “+ More” button at the bottom of the gray left hand menu. Sometimes there are additional tabs that are hidden in this section.

I hope this info helps!
